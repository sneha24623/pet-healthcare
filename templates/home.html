🐾 Amazing Pet Facts

🐕

Super Sniffers

Dogs have 300 million olfactory receptors compared to humans' 6 million. They can detect scents at concentrations nearly 100 million times lower than humans can!

🐱

Cat Communication

Cats have over 100 vocal sounds, while dogs only have about 10. Adult cats rarely meow at other cats - they developed meowing specifically to communicate with humans!

❤️

Heart Helpers

Pet ownership can lower blood pressure, reduce stress hormones, and decrease the risk of heart disease. Just 15 minutes with a pet can release feel-good hormones!

🧠

Smart Companions

Dogs can learn over 150 words and can count up to 4 or 5. Border Collies are considered the smartest breed, capable of learning up to 1,000 words!

😴

Sleep Champions

Cats sleep 12-16 hours per day, while dogs sleep 12-14 hours. Puppies and senior dogs need even more sleep - up to 18-20 hours daily!

🏃

Speed Demons

Greyhounds can reach speeds up to 45 mph, making them the fastest dog breed. Cats can run up to 30 mph and can jump up to 6 times their length!
